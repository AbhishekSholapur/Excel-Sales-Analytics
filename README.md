# Excel Sales & Finance Analytics

A comprehensive analytics project demonstrating end-to-end data analysis, visualization, and business intelligence using Excel, Power Query, Power Pivot, and DAX.

---

## 📊 Project Overview

This project showcases the development of professional business intelligence reports for sales and financial performance monitoring, designed to empower stakeholders with actionable insights for strategic decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Sales Analytics Report

### Objectives
1. **[Customer Performance Report](https://github.com/AbhishekSholapur/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/Customer%20Performance%20Report.pdf)** – Analyze individual customer contributions to revenue and profitability
2. **[Market Performance vs Target Report](https://github.com/AbhishekSholapur/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/Market%20Performance%20vs%20Target%20Report.pdf)** – Evaluate sales execution against strategic targets across regions and markets

### Key Insights & Purpose
- Monitor and evaluate sales activities in real-time
- Identify seasonal patterns and sales trends
- Track critical performance indicators (KPIs) to optimize business outcomes

### Business Impact
- **Customer Strategy:** Determine optimal discount structures for improved customer relationships
- **Negotiation Support:** Leverage data-driven insights for customer negotiations
- **Growth Opportunities:** Identify high-potential markets for expansion and resource allocation

## 💰 Finance Analytics Report

### Objectives
1. **Profit & Loss Statements**
   - [P&L by Fiscal Year](https://github.com/AbhishekSholapur/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/P%26L%20Statement%20by%20Fiscal%20Year.pdf)
   - [P&L by Months](https://github.com/AbhishekSholapur/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/P%26L%20Statement%20by%20Months.pdf)
2. **[Market-wise P&L Analysis](https://github.com/AbhishekSholapur/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/P%26L%20Statement%20by%20Markets.pdf)** – Compare profitability across different geographic and product markets

### Key Insights & Purpose
- Evaluate financial health and operational efficiency
- Support executive decision-making with quantitative benchmarks
- Facilitate transparent stakeholder communication on financial performance

### Business Impact
- **Performance Benchmarking:** Compare results against industry peers and historical performance
- **Strategic Planning:** Foundation for budgeting, forecasting, and resource optimization
- **Stakeholder Confidence:** Demonstrate fiscal responsibility and growth trajectory
